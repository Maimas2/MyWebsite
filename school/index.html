LaTeX Class for Bills

It's not a good class; there's no documentation or options or anything like that. There's a small example file included with it (originally made for Waterford Model Congress), but that's all. In my arrogant opinion, it didn't come out too shabby.

Leechfield Constitution

Working at June Norcross Webster Scout Reservation over the summer, the area of Staff Row I lived in became a small democratic government, and I made a short constitution to outline the government. It is tailored for our group of maybe eight people; there is really only one branch and everything was exclusively self-governing. Still, it was a really fun project to do, and I hope that someday I'll make another constitution for an actual country, not just an eight-person direct democracy. The physical document is typeset in LaTeX using a modified version of the bill class above.

Carillon by Georges Bizet

We played the First Arlesienne Suite by Georges Bizet at Connecticut Eastern Regions Festival my freshman year. That is an amazing suite of music, and I loved every moment of it, especially the fourth movement Carillon. A while later, I was wanting to learn how to use LMMS, and decided to try to recreate Carillon in it (LMMS is not meant to be used for Classical music, you'll note). It took about an afternoon, much quicker than I thought, although in fairness it only includes the second half of the movement.

Dominion card designer

Dominion by Donald X. Vaccarino is a really fun game and I would recommend it to everyone. There is a good card designer here (which is, has been, and will be better than mine), but I wanted to try to make my own as a desktop app. It is made with GLFW and OpenGL, while also using cURL and Freetype. All written in C++, the goated language. This is an older project, and I never set it up to really work on other systems. After all, "it works on my machine!" It doesn't really work on Windows at all, and I have no clue about MacOS or anything else. I made it on my small Linux Mint laptop and never looked back.
