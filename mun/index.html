Help / About / Setting(s)


I made this website for my high school's Capstone project (though I have drastically added to it since). It was specifically made for use in my school's Model UN club, where I am on the dais this year. I intend for it to supplement the traditional in-person conference, not supplant it.


Any ideas to improve the site, any ideas at all, are entirely welcome! You can submit them through the Annoy Me portal or email me at ASeltz156 at gmail.com.


This website is open-source under the GNU GPLv3 License. The source code is hosted on GitHub.


This website uses jQuery; SortableJS for the sortable lists; and jQuery-Sortable for jQuery bindings.


Country flags were gratefully taken from https://flagpedia.net.


It is recommended to use this website on a screen that is at least 1920x1080. It may look weird at larger resolutions, but also may be inoperable at smaller ones.
